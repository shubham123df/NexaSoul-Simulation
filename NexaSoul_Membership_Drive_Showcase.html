Your complete student journey

From curious
to career-ready.

One membership. A visible path. Every stage gives you a meaningful next step—so you always know what to learn, build, and aim for.

Foundation

Find your starting point: coding basics, tools, Git, and a learning routine that lasts.

You will walk away with: Git workflow, VS Code mastery, basic Python/JS
Time commitment: 4-6 hours/week
Projects: 2 mini projects

Learning cohorts

Explore focused tracks with weekly sessions, practice, and support from peers.

You will walk away with: Deep dive into your chosen track
Time commitment: 6-8 hours/week
Projects: 3 focused projects

Project builder

Turn your knowledge into mini and major projects you can present with confidence.

You will walk away with: Portfolio-ready projects
Time commitment: 8-10 hours/week
Projects: 1 major project + 2 mini

Collaborate & compete

Join teams for hackathons, open-source contributions, events, and real deadlines.

You will walk away with: Team collaboration skills
Time commitment: 10-12 hours/week
Projects: Hackathon participation + OSS

Industry preparation

Strengthen your resume, interview skills, communication, and professional profile.

You will walk away with: Job-ready profile
Time commitment: 6-8 hours/week
Projects: Resume + LinkedIn + Portfolio

Leadership & mentorship

Lead initiatives, mentor newcomers, and create a community impact that outlives you.

You will walk away with: Leadership experience
Time commitment: 4-6 hours/week
Projects: Lead a team/mentor juniors
